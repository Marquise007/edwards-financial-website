Meeting notes

Read this as a lens, not a cage. Pompian’s types are regions on continuous dimensions, not fixed boxes — real clients blend, sit near boundaries, and shift with circumstance. A near-boundary result names two types for a reason. Re-assess at life transitions (retirement, inheritance, business sale) and major market dislocations, and triangulate this read against biography and observed behavior rather than trusting any single instrument.
