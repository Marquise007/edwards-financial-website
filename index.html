What We Do

Practice
Areas

A full spectrum of advanced financial strategies, each tailored precisely, never templated.

01

Retirement Strategy

Income strategies built around the retirement you actually want. They turn what you’ve accumulated into dependable, lasting cash flow, with protection and long-term-care planning built in. A health event late in life shouldn’t undo what you spent decades building.

02

Estate & Legacy Planning

Advanced planning that works in concert with your attorney and CPA. Trust and insurance structures move wealth across generations as efficiently as the law allows, and direct part of what you’ve built toward the causes and community you care about.

03

Premium Financing

A strategy for qualified high-net-worth clients. Third-party financing funds substantial life insurance, while your capital stays deployed where it already serves you. The structure stays conservative and coordinated with your existing plan.

04

Asset-Based Liquidity

Strategies that put the assets you’ve already built to work. A private reserve strengthens protection and liquidity for your estate, while your broader plan stays intact. Everything stays coordinated with your existing advisors.

05

Tax-Advantaged Accumulation

Accumulation strategies that pursue growth with downside protection and tax advantages, and can supplement retirement income. Results depend on the structure and policy selected.

06

Business Succession & Key-Person Planning

For owners whose company is their largest asset. The plan funds buy-sell agreements, insures the people the business can’t replace, and structures an exit that honors a lifetime of building.

Our Specialty

Pension
Optimization

You spent a career earning it. Yet the decisions that turn a pension into a lifetime of security are made once, and most can never be undone. This is the work this practice was built for.

Run Your Pension Numbers Begin the Conversation
01

The Election & Survivor Decision

The permanent choice between a larger check today and protection for the spouse who may outlive you. The decision is modeled clearly, with intention, before the form is ever signed.

02

The Pension Gap

The distance between what your pension replaces and what your retirement actually costs. That gap is quantified honestly, then closed with the right complementary strategies.

03

Social Security Coordination

When to claim, and how it sequences alongside your pension. These timing decisions quietly determine how much of the retirement you earned you actually keep.

04

Pension Taxation

Pension income is generally taxed as ordinary income federally, and states differ sharply: California taxes it in full; others, like Florida, not at all. How and where you draw matters.
